Logitech - Accounts Payable Accountant

Project Overview

During my time as an Accounts Payable Accountant at Logitech, I was responsible for managing accounts payable and providing financial support for cash forecasting. I conducted extensive analysis on employee expense claims to streamline the financial processes and improve the accuracy of forecasts.

Key Responsibilities

  • Reviewed and processed employee expense claims, ensuring compliance with company policies.
  • Supported the cash forecasting process by analyzing current and future accounts payable data.
  • Identified discrepancies in expense reporting and worked with cross-functional teams to resolve issues.

Impact

My efforts resulted in the identification of significant discrepancies in long-term employee expenses, which were addressed by revising processes to ensure more accurate expense reporting moving forward. This led to more efficient cash forecasting and better financial visibility for the company.
